Our Certificate and online programmes

Certificates and diplomas are each designed for completion within two years, making them both shorter alternatives to the associate degree. They are a popular educational pathway among high school graduates who wish to receive relevant job training and enter the workforce without completing a full degree program, as well as established professionals seeking higher salaries and more advancement opportunities.


The online programmes are taught through remote engagement learning.
This makes it easier for the student and the tutor since it is flexible and can be learnt and
taught at the comfort of ones home.


These online programmes are usually conducted by the help of zoom meetings and google meet.
